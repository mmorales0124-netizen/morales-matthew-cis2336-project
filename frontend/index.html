Welcome

You found us. That's the first sign you belong here.

ArtConnect exists for the artists whose work doesn't quite fit the white cube — the ones drawing from static, scripture, and scene flyers in equal measure. We built a place to show that work, sell it if you want to, and find the people who are already looking for it.

Every piece in this gallery was made by someone still figuring out how to say the thing they can't say out loud. We just give it somewhere to hang.

"Art doesn't need to be comfortable to be honest."
